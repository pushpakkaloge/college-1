Research and Development

"Take up one idea! Make that idea in your life, think of it. Dream of it and live on that idea. Let the brain, muscles, nerves every part of your body be full of that idea and just leave every other idea alone. This is the way to success", said Swami Vivekananda. This is the "Success Mantra" for the Research & Innovations for the faculties as well as students.


Vision

Develop a research culture and stimulate an environment that nurtures and rewards innovation, creativity, discovery and collaboration


Mission

  • To provide infrastructure and training facilities that promotes research, innovation, creativity and entrepreneurship
  • To execute research activities to get acquainted with newer technological trends.
  • To enhance collaborations between researchers, government & research organizations and industries.
